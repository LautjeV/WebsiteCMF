Charleston
Manor
Festival

Summer and Charleston were made for each other. Each year the great British cellist Robert Cohen invites friends and colleagues - some of the most oustanding musicians before the public today - to join him for the intense yet congenial music-making that is Charleston Manor Festival.

Announcement: Due to major renovations at Charleston Manor, the Festival will not take place in 2014. Please check back here for an update on the resumption of the Festival. We look forward to welcoming you back another year!
